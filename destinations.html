Horton Plains

Horton Plains is a stunning highland national park in Sri Lanka, known for its cool climate, misty landscapes, and rich biodiversity. It features scenic trails through cloud forests and grasslands, leading to the famous World’s End cliff, which offers breathtaking panoramic views. The park is also home to unique wildlife, waterfalls, and rare plants, making it a favorite spot for nature lovers and hikers.

Ohiya

Ohiya is a peaceful mountain village in Sri Lanka, nestled in the central highlands. Known for its cool climate and lush greenery, it serves as a gateway to Horton Plains National Park. Ohiya is popular among travelers seeking tranquility, scenic train rides, tea plantations, and beautiful hiking trails through misty hills and waterfalls.

Idalgashinna

Idalgashinna is a serene village in Sri Lanka’s central highlands, famous for its stunning views and mist-covered tea plantations. It’s a peaceful spot ideal for nature lovers and hikers, offering easy access to scenic trails and the picturesque railway line known for its beautiful bridges and tunnels. Idalgashinna’s tranquil atmosphere makes it a perfect escape from the bustle of city life.
